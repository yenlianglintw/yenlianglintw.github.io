About me

I am currently a research scientist at A9.com, Amazon. Before that, I was a computer vision scientist at GE Global Research Center (GRC). I received my PhD in Computer Science at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Prof. Winston Hsu. From 2013/06 ~ 2014/03, I had research visiting at University of Maryland advised by Prof. Larry S. Davis and Prof. Vlad I. Morariu.
