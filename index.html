About me

I am an applied scientist at Amazon A9 working on visual search. Before that, I was a computer vision scientist at GE Global Research. I received my PhD in Computer Science at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Prof. Winston Hsu. I had a research visit at University of Maryland advised by Prof. Larry S. Davis and Prof. Vlad I. Morariu.
