About me

I am currently a computer vision scientist at GE Global Research Center (GRC). My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I received my PhD in Computer Science at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Prof. Winston Hsu. From 2013/06 ~ 2014/03, I had research visiting at University of Maryland advised by Prof. Larry S. Davis and Prof. Vlad I. Morariu.
